# FL-23 Voting Procedures Dashboard

Static dashboard build for Florida's 23rd Congressional District (2026 cycle).

## Critical redistricting nuance

Under **Florida HB 1-D** (signed May 4, 2026), Florida's congressional district numbers were reshuffled mid-decade. For the 2026 election cycle:

- **NEW FL-23** = the territory formerly known as **FL-22** — **Rep. Lois Frankel's** Palm Beach County coastal seat (West Palm Beach → Boca Raton).
- **OLD FL-23** (Jared Moskowitz's Broward/Boca Raton seat) was renumbered **FL-25** under HB 1-D.

The new FL-23 is **safer** for Democrats than the old FL-22 (approx. D+13 vs. D+7 prior Cook PVI) because HB 1-D removed western Palm Beach County (more Republican) from the district. Cook, Inside Elections, Sabato's Crystal Ball, and The Economist all rate the new FL-23 as **Safe/Solid D**.

Mar-a-Lago (Trump's Florida residence) sits on Palm Beach Island inside FL-23.

The Florida Supreme Court denied the emergency petition in *Equal Ground Education Fund v. Byrd* 6-1 on June 10, 2026 — the HB 1-D map is locked for both the August 18 primary and November 3 general election.

## Data caveats

- **Ballotpedia's FL-23 page is stale** — it still shows the old FL-23 (Moskowitz-era) candidates. Use Wikipedia's 2026 Florida U.S. House page or the Florida DOS general election candidate list instead.
- **FL DOS Frankel filing and FEC committee both still read "District 22"** as a pre-HB-1-D paperwork artifact. She is running in the new FL-23.
